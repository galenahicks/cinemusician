Cinemusician is an interactive music experience intended to highlight the similarities between music creation and visual art
It does this by allowing you to break down songs and videos by attaching each instrument part to a video layer
Each slider controls the opacity of a video layer as well as the volume of the corresponding instrument part; as you adjust each channel, you become the music producer as well as the cinematographer. After one loop, song and video might un-sync; if so, press "reload"
